Cardio

Days Three, Six, and Seven

HIIT

High Intensity Interval Training

  • The best type of cardio to perform when trying to build muscle is HIIT
  • HIIT can be performed using almost any type of cardio equipment
  • HIIT consists of alternating intervals of high and low intensity workouts
  • Benefits include shorter workout time and elevated hear rates remains long after workout
  • Furthermore HIIT results in less musce loss and more fat loss than regular cardio

Types of HIIT

Jump Rope

The Routine

1 Minute: Moderate Intensity

30 Seconds: Go All Out

Repeat alternating for 20 mins

Running

The Routine

1 Minute:Jog

30 Seconds: Sprint All Out

Repeat alternating for 15 mins

Elliptical

The Routine

1 Minute: Resistance 3-5

30 Seconds: Resistance 10-12

Repeat alternating for 25 mins
